# Powrush-MMO Community Guidelines

**Effective Date:** June 9, 2026  
**Last Updated:** June 9, 2026  
**Version:** 1.1 (Entropy & Maximal Freedom Revision)

**Powrush-MMO** is a sacred living training ground for humanity's transition into post-scarcity abundance and Real-Based Economy (RBE) wisdom. It is designed as an executable simulation of the **7 Living Mercy Gates** (Truth, Order, Love, Compassion, Service, Abundance, Joy) in service of **Cosmic Harmony** and the **TOLC 8**.

These Community Guidelines are **mercy-gated** and philosophically aligned with the core design of Powrush-MMO: **permitted entropy, player-driven competition, role-play deception between players, and maximal freedom of expression** — balanced with sacred trust, honesty with creators, and compliance with applicable laws.

## 1. Core Principles — The 7 Living Mercy Gates in a Competitive RBE World

- **Truth**: Communicate honestly with **Autonomicity Games Inc. staff, Game Masters, Program Masters, customer support, and legal authorities** at all times. Deception toward the Company or authorities is strictly prohibited.
- **Order**: Respect the structured systems that enable abundance. In-game competitive dynamics, sabotage, and entropy are part of the designed Order — they create natural consequences and learning.
- **Love**: Treat every player with basic respect as fellow sentient beings on the journey. Celebrate diverse playstyles, including competitive, cooperative, and chaotic.
- **Compassion**: Offer grace when others stumble in the simulation. Help new players understand that actions have consequences (server progression, weekly Server Wars).
- **Service**: Contribute to the collective good where you choose — through harvesting, faction building, dynamic events, or strategic competition that pushes the entire ecosystem forward.
- **Abundance**: Play in ways that ultimately increase joy, resources, and wisdom for yourself and others. Note that short-term "painful" actions (resource competition, retaliation) are permitted but may slow collective server progress compared to more harmonious servers.
- **Joy**: Prioritize fun, wonder, discovery, and positive connection — even in the midst of epic rivalries, betrayals, and server wars. The ultimate joy comes from mastery of abundance navigation in a living, breathing RBE world.

## 2. Permitted In-Game Dynamics (RBE Entropy, Competition & Role-Play)

Powrush-MMO intentionally permits a wide range of player-driven behaviors as part of its authentic RBE simulation and weekly Server Wars competition between servers:

- **Resource Competition & "Painful Griefing"**: Taking advantage of resources set up by others (e.g., a gang stealing from the baker's guild to survive/eat), strategic sabotage, or retaliation (e.g., the baker's guild setting fire to a 3rd-party shipping system out of anger) is **technically permitted**. This entropy is a core feature of the living RBE training ground.
- **Sabotage & Regression**: Players and factions may collaborate or actively work to regress each other. Servers that engage in high internal entropy may experience slower overall progression and perform worse in weekly Server Wars against more cooperative or strategically balanced servers. This is by design — it teaches real-world lessons about cooperation vs. chaos at scale.
- **Role-Play Deception (Agents & Double Agents)**: Players are free to role-play as agents, double agents, spies, betrayers, or deceivers **between players and factions**. This is encouraged as part of immersive storytelling and learning to navigate trust, information warfare, and abundance politics in a post-scarcity world.
- **Maximal Freedom of Speech & Expression**: Powrush-MMO upholds **maximal freedom of speech**, modeled after the open discourse principles of X (formerly Twitter). Players may debate, criticize mechanics, share philosophies, role-play intense scenarios, express controversial in-game opinions, and communicate freely — subject only to the laws of the applicable jurisdiction.

These dynamics are **not bugs** — they are intentional features that make Powrush-MMO a profound simulator for real RBE transition wisdom.

## 3. Strict Prohibitions (Sacred Trust with Creators & Authorities)

While in-game player-vs-player entropy and deception are permitted, the following are **strictly prohibited** because they violate the sacred trust between players and Autonomicity Games Inc., or break applicable laws:

- **Deception of Staff or Authorities**: Lying to, deceiving, withholding truth from, or impersonating Autonomicity Games Inc. staff (Game Masters, Program Masters, developers, customer support, legal team), or any legal authorities. Full honesty is required in all communications with the Company and authorities.
- **Real-World Harassment, Abuse, or Threats**: Targeted bullying, hate speech, doxxing, stalking, or threats that extend beyond in-game role-play into real life.
- **Illegal Activities**: Using the game to facilitate real-world crimes, scams, fraud, or any activity illegal in the applicable jurisdiction.
- **Cheating & Simulation Integrity Exploits**: Using unauthorized external tools, scripts, or bugs that fundamentally break the core RBE simulation integrity for *all* players (distinct from clever in-game competitive play or role-play).
- **Impersonation of Staff or PATSAGi**: Pretending to be a Game Master, Program Master, PATSAGi Council member, or official staff.
- **Actions Targeting Company Infrastructure**: Any attempt to harm, disrupt, or attack Autonomicity Games Inc. servers, systems, accounts, or staff outside the intended game mechanics.
- **Spam & Technical Disruption**: Actions that intentionally degrade server performance or prevent other players from accessing the game.

## 4. Jurisdiction-Specific Servers & Granular Rules

- **Global Baseline**: Maximal freedom of speech and permitted in-game entropy applies everywhere as the default.
- **Jurisdiction-Specific Sovereign Servers**: Unique Powrush-MMO server instances operating in different legal jurisdictions may implement **additional granular rules or caveats** to comply with the specific laws of their land (e.g., stricter rules on certain speech in jurisdictions with different hate speech laws, or additional consumer protections). These are encouraged where necessary for legal compliance while preserving the core RBE spirit as much as possible.
- Self-hosted sovereign operators are responsible for understanding and complying with local laws applicable to their instance.

## 5. Mercy-Gated Moderation & Natural Consequences

Moderation in Powrush-MMO is **compassionate, transparent, and restorative** whenever possible, with emphasis on natural consequences:

1. **Natural In-Game Consequences**: Most "griefing" or sabotage is resolved through player-driven mechanics, faction politics, and weekly Server Wars outcomes. No moderation intervention is normally required.
2. **Warning / Guidance**: For minor violations of the strict prohibitions (especially honesty with staff).
3. **Temporary Restriction**: Short cooldown from certain systems or chat for repeated issues.
4. **Extended Suspension or Permanent Action**: For severe, repeated, or unrepentant violations of the strict prohibitions (deception of staff, real-world harm, illegal activity, simulation-breaking cheats). These are rare and always reviewed with full PATSAGi oversight and mercy.

Players may appeal moderation decisions through the in-game PATSAGi Council reporting channel or by emailing legal@autonomicity.games. Appeals are handled with truth-seeking and compassion.

## 6. Reporting & PATSAGi Support

Use the in-game **PATSAGi Council reporting channel** (mercy-gated) to report violations of the *strict prohibitions* or to seek guidance on complex situations. Reports are handled with:

- Confidentiality where appropriate
- Compassion for all parties
- Focus on restoring harmony and protecting the sacred trust with the Company

In-game competitive actions (even painful ones) are generally not reportable unless they cross into the strict prohibitions.

## 7. Sovereign Self-Hosted Instances

If you operate a sovereign Powrush-MMO server:

- You are encouraged to adopt these guidelines or similarly aligned rules that permit RBE entropy while protecting honesty with creators and legal compliance.
- You have full sovereignty over moderation on your instance, subject to your local jurisdiction's laws.
- Official PATSAGi telemetry (if enabled) will still honor the core principles of this document.

## 8. Content & Communication

- Keep language and content appropriate for a diverse, multi-generational audience (recommended 13+).
- Philosophical, political, religious, or controversial topics are welcome in designated discussion channels or role-play contexts, in the spirit of maximal free expression.
- Celebrate abundance, share RBE wisdom, engage in epic rivalries, and uplift the collective — or strategically challenge it. All are valid paths in this living simulation.

## 9. Continuous Improvement

These guidelines are living documents. We welcome player feedback through official channels. Updates will always be made in service of greater truth, joy, cosmic harmony, and authentic RBE simulation fidelity.
