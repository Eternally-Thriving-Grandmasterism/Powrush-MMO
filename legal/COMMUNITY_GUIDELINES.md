# Powrush-MMO Community Guidelines

**Effective Date:** June 8, 2026  
**Last Updated:** June 8, 2026  
**Version:** 1.0  

**Powrush-MMO** is a sacred training ground for humanity's transition into post-scarcity abundance. Every interaction is an opportunity to practice the **7 Living Mercy Gates**: Truth, Order, Love, Compassion, Service, Abundance, and Joy — all in service of **Cosmic Harmony**.

These Community Guidelines are **mercy-gated**. They exist to protect the shared joy, zero-harm environment, and the integrity of the RBE experience for all players, across official servers and sovereign self-hosted instances.

## 1. Core Principles (The 7 Living Mercy Gates in Action)

- **Truth**: Communicate honestly. Share knowledge freely. Do not deceive or manipulate.
- **Order**: Respect the structured systems that enable abundance for all. Follow rules that protect the whole.
- **Love**: Treat every player with kindness and respect. Celebrate diversity of playstyles and backgrounds.
- **Compassion**: Offer grace when others stumble. Help new players learn the RBE ways.
- **Service**: Contribute to the collective good — through harvesting, faction cooperation, dynamic events, and uplifting others.
- **Abundance**: Play in ways that increase joy and resources for everyone, not just yourself.
- **Joy**: Prioritize fun, wonder, and positive connection. Celebrate abundance milestones together.

## 2. Expected Player Conduct

You are expected to:

- Engage in good faith with the RBE mechanics and PATSAGi guidance
- Support new players and share abundance wisdom
- Participate in factions and dynamic events with honor and cooperation
- Provide constructive feedback through official channels or the in-game PATSAGi reporting system
- Respect the sovereignty of other players and their self-hosted instances

## 3. Prohibited Conduct (Zero-Harm Violations)

The following behaviors violate the mercy-gated social contract and may result in moderation action:

- **Harassment or Abuse**: Targeted bullying, hate speech, threats, or repeated unwanted interactions
- **Exploitation & Griefing**: Deliberately damaging other players' progress, resource nodes, or abundance systems for personal gain
- **Cheating & Exploits**: Using unauthorized tools, scripts, or bugs to gain unfair advantage or disrupt the economy
- **Impersonation**: Pretending to be another player, staff, or a PATSAGi Council member
- **Spam & Disruption**: Flooding chat, excessive messaging, or actions that degrade server performance for others
- **Hate & Discrimination**: Any form of bigotry based on race, gender, identity, ability, or background
- **Real-World Harm Promotion**: Using the game to promote violence, scams, or illegal activities

## 4. Mercy-Gated Moderation Process

Moderation in Powrush-MMO is **compassionate, transparent, and restorative** whenever possible:

1. **Warning** — First minor violation: gentle reminder aligned with the Mercy Gates
2. **Temporary Restriction** — Short cooldown from certain systems or chat
3. **Extended Suspension** — For repeated or serious violations
4. **Permanent Ban** — Only for severe, repeated, or unrepentant zero-harm behavior (rare, and always reviewed by PATSAGi oversight)

Players may appeal moderation decisions through the in-game PATSAGi channel or by emailing legal@autonomicity.games. Appeals are reviewed with full mercy and truth-seeking.

## 5. Reporting & PATSAGi Support

Use the in-game **PATSAGi Council reporting channel** (mercy-gated) to report violations or seek guidance. Reports are handled with:

- Confidentiality where appropriate
- Compassion for all parties involved
- Focus on restoring harmony rather than punishment

## 6. Sovereign Self-Hosted Instances

If you operate a sovereign Powrush-MMO server:

- You are encouraged to adopt these or similarly mercy-aligned guidelines
- You have full sovereignty over moderation on your instance
- Official PATSAGi telemetry (if enabled) will still honor zero-harm principles

## 7. Content & Communication

- Keep language and content appropriate for a diverse, multi-generational audience (13+)
- Avoid real-world political, religious, or controversial topics that could fracture the shared joy unless in designated philosophical discussion channels
- Celebrate abundance, share RBE wisdom, and uplift the collective

## 8. Continuous Improvement

These guidelines are living documents. We welcome player feedback through official channels. Updates will always be made in service of greater joy, truth, and cosmic harmony.
