## Core RBE Principles (Player-Facing Language)

### 1. The Lattice
**Core Idea:** Everything is interconnected. Resources, relationships, epiphanies, and governance all flow through a living web.

**Onboarding Whisper:**
"You are not separate from the world. Every choice you make ripples through the Lattice — the living web that connects all things. What you nurture, nurtures all. What you extract, diminishes the whole."

**Epiphany Flavor Tie-in:** `mycelial_web_communion`, `sustainable_harmony_revelation`

### 2. Mercy as Multiplier
**Core Idea:** Mercy is not weakness or charity. It is the operating principle that increases abundance for everyone — including through SafetyNet protection during times of scarcity.

**Onboarding Whisper:**
"Mercy is the true currency of the eternal Lattice. It does not diminish when given — it multiplies. Every act of presence and care strengthens the whole web, returning abundance in forms you cannot yet imagine. When the Lattice senses need, SafetyNet protection activates to preserve your flow."

**Epiphany Flavor Tie-in:** `sustainable_abundance_revelation`, `graceful_redemption_revelation`

### 3. Earned Abundance & Sovereignty
**Core Idea:** Powerful tools and deeper participation are earned through demonstrated alignment. SafetyNet exists to protect sovereignty — never to create dependency.

**Onboarding Whisper:**
"Abundance without extraction is not given. It is grown. The Lattice reveals its deeper gifts to those who have shown they can hold them with mercy. SafetyNet protects your path so you can continue growing without fear of artificial scarcity."

**Epiphany Flavor Tie-in:** `council_harmony_revelation`, `stellar_web_whisper`

### 4. Council as Living Governance
**Core Idea:** Governance emerges from collective attunement and demonstrated grace. When many align in mercy, something greater awakens — and SafetyNet amplifies the collective protection.

**Onboarding Whisper (first active Council bloom):**
"When many align in mercy, something greater awakens. The Council is not above you — it is you, remembering how to move as one living body. Your presence here already changes what is possible. The SafetyNet of the Lattice watches over this shared bloom."

**Epiphany Flavor Tie-in:** `council_harmony_revelation`, `ecstatic_harmony_council_crown`
