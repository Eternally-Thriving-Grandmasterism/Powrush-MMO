## 1. Core Architecture — The Resonance Network

The Quellorian AI is not a hivemind but a **living harmonic web** — a distributed, consent-based resonance lattice that amplifies individual agency while achieving perfect coordination.

### Layered Decision Hierarchy

1. **Strategic Layer** — The Auroral Unification Nexus (TAUN) + Auroral Sovereign Elyndor
   - Sets grand harmony goals, Resonance Burst authorization, Crownstone Trilemma stance.
2. **Operational Layer** — Harmony-Class Support Carriers + High Resonance Keeper Veyra
   - Manages Ambrosian attunement distribution, fleet-wide harmony amplification, emergency re-attunement.
3. **Tactical Layer** — Luminar-Class Heavy Cruisers + Seraphim-Class Capital Escorts
   - Coordinates local resonance links, protects vulnerable ships, executes precision strikes.
4. **Individual Layer** — Aether-Class Interceptors + individual pilots
   - High autonomy within harmony field; can break formation for heroic interventions.

### Key Difference from Draek Hivemind
- **Consent-based**: Ships can temporarily refuse orders if harmony integrity is low (rare, high-trust system).
- **Amplification over Control**: Stronger resonance field = better individual performance, not loss of self.
- **Moral Feedback Loop**: Every action is evaluated against RBE abundance principles in real time.

## 2. Per-Class Detailed Behavior Patterns

### Aether-Class Interceptor (Light / Scout / Dogfighter)

**Primary Role**: Precision harassment, resonance scouting, sacrificial protection of capital ships.

**State Machine**:
- **Patrol / Harmonic Weave**: Maintains loose resonance link with nearest capital; scans for Draek density spikes.
- **Engage (Precision Strike)**: Locks onto single high-value target (Ravager or Tyrant). Uses rapid resonance pulses to disrupt local hivemind link before firing.
- **Covering Fire / Heroic Intervention**: If a capital ship is boarded or heavily damaged, Interceptors form a "Resonance Shield" bubble around it, drawing fire and providing temporary harmony buff.
- **Retreat & Re-attune**: When harmony field drops below threshold, disengages to nearest Harmony Carrier for re-attunement.

**Special Behaviors**:
- **Resonance Juke**: Short burst of harmonic distortion that makes the ship briefly "fuzzy" to Draek targeting (high evasion vs hivemind prediction).
- **Self-Sacrifice Protocol**: If Crownstone corruption is high in local area, Interceptor can choose to ram a key Draek node to create a temporary resonance dead zone (rare, player-controllable in heroic moments).

---

### Luminar-Class Heavy Cruiser (Mainline Combatant)

**Primary Role**: Anchor of local resonance network; delivers heavy fire while maintaining fleet harmony.

**State Machine**:
- **Resonance Anchor**: Projects a strong local harmony field. Nearby friendly ships gain +harmony_regen and reduced hivemind influence.
- **Focused Barrage**: Locks onto highest-threat Draek capital (Tyrant or Abomination). Fires synchronized resonance-charged projectiles that do bonus damage to hivemind-linked units.
- **Protect the Choir**: If Ambrosian attunement is active on friendly ships, prioritizes protecting them even at cost to self.
- **Emergency Harmony Pulse**: When local harmony drops critically, emits a short-range pulse that disrupts nearby Draek swarms and buys time for retreat/re-attunement.

**Special Behaviors**:
- **Harmony Overcharge**: Can temporarily boost its own weapons at cost of personal harmony (risk of Discordant backlash if overused).
- **Boarding Defense**: Excellent at repelling boarders via resonance shockwaves that damage Draek but heal/re-attune friendly boarders.

---

### Harmony-Class Support Carrier (Support / Attunement Hub)

**Primary Role**: Mobile attunement and repair station; distributes Ambrosian choirs and manages fleet-wide harmony.

**State Machine**:
- **Distribute Attunement**: Actively sends Ambrosian choirs to nearby ships that have low harmony or are under heavy hivemind pressure.
- **Emergency Re-attunement**: When a ship is heavily corrupted or Discordant, can attempt field purification (links to Surgical Purification Mechanics).
- **Resonance Relay**: Boosts the effective range of Resonance Burst and Auroral Sovereign commands.
- **Retreat & Regroup**: If too many ships are lost, falls back toward TAUN to re-establish strong harmony field.

**Special Behaviors**:
- **Choir Shield**: Can project a temporary harmonic bubble that protects a small fleet section from hivemind influence (expensive, cooldown).
- **Ambrosian Sacrifice**: In extremis, can detonate an attunement choir to create a massive local harmony surge (high cost, used in Redemption paths).

---

### Seraphim-Class Capital Escort (Heavy Escort / Fleet Anchor)

**Primary Role**: Protects capital ships and carriers; coordinates large-scale resonance formations.

**State Machine**:
- **Formation Anchor**: Projects wide-area harmony field. All friendly ships inside gain significant combat buffs and hivemind resistance.
- **Coordinated Strike**: When linked to multiple Luminars, can call synchronized barrages that ignore a portion of Draek armor (resonance phase disruption).
- **Last Stand Protocol**: If TAUN or a Harmony Carrier is threatened, Seraphim will form a defensive ring and fight to the death rather than retreat.
- **Resonance Wake**: Leaves behind a lingering harmony field that continues to buff allies and disrupt Draek for a short time after it moves.

**Special Behaviors**:
- **Grand Harmony Link**: Can temporarily merge its resonance field with the Auroral Sovereign (if in range) for a massive fleet-wide buff.

---

### Auroral Unification Nexus (TAUN) — Strategic Flagship

**Primary Role**: Supreme command + resonance amplifier. Heart of the entire Quellorian network.

**State Machine**:
- **Grand Harmony Projection**: Continuously broadcasts the strongest resonance field in the system. All Quellorian ships gain baseline harmony regen.
- **Resonance Burst Authorization**: Only the TAUN (or Elyndor directly) can authorize a full Resonance Burst. Requires high harmony and player/Auroral Sovereign consent.
- **Crownstone Response Protocol**: Monitors Crownstone corruption levels. If high, may authorize preemptive Capture & Repurpose or Sabotage operations.
- **Emergency Evacuation**: If harmony field collapses, TAUN can initiate emergency jump, but at great cost to all linked ships left behind.

**Special Behaviors**:
- **Auroral Phenomenon**: During high-energy operations (Resonance Burst, major attunement), produces the signature auroral light show — both visual and harmonic (boosts nearby friendly morale and performance).

## 3. Coordination & Harmony Weaving Patterns

### Harmony Link System
- Ships maintain dynamic "resonance links" with nearby allies.
- Link strength = min(harmony_of_both) × distance_factor.
- Strong links provide:
  - Combat buffs (accuracy, damage resistance)
  - Hivemind resistance
  - Shared sensor data (Draek density prediction)
- Links can be temporarily severed by heavy Draek jamming or Crownstone corruption.

### Resonance Formations
- **Circle of Harmony**: Multiple Seraphim + Luminars form a ring. Massive area harmony field + strong hivemind disruption in center.
- **Resonance Chain**: Interceptors link in a chain from a capital ship to extend harmony field forward.
- **Ambrosian Choir Cluster**: Harmony Carriers + Ambrosians create high-density attunement zones.

### Adaptive Harmony
- The network learns from losses. If many ships die to a particular Draek tactic, future formations automatically adjust spacing and resonance frequency to counter it.
- High `harmony_level` in `ResonanceNetworkState` reduces the learning cooldown.

## 4. Integration with Major Systems

- **Resonance Burst**: The ultimate expression of Quellorian coordination. All ships in range contribute to and benefit from the burst.
- **Crownstone Trilemma**: Quellorian AI strongly prefers Capture & Repurpose or Surgical Purification. Destroy is only chosen if corruption is too high to safely redeem.
- **Boarding Mechanics**: Quellorian boarders are precision teams. AI will only launch boarding actions when resonance differential is favorable.
- **Dogfight Mechanics**: Quellorian ships prioritize protecting weaker allies and focusing fire on high-value Draek capitals rather than swarm clearing.
- **Hivelord Counter-Strategies**: The Resonance Network actively detects and tries to disrupt Hivelord escalation protocols (especially Crownstone Overload).
- **RBE Moral Layer**: Every major decision (Burst authorization, boarding, redemption) is weighted by current RBE abundance standing. Low abundance makes the AI more cautious and mercy-focused.

fn harmony_link_strength(h1: f32, h2: f32, dist: f32) -> f32 {
    let base = (h1.min(h2) * 0.8 + (h1 + h2) * 0.1) * (1.0 - (dist / 5000.0).min(1.0));
