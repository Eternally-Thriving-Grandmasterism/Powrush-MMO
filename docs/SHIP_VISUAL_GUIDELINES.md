# Ship Visual Guidelines — Powrush-MMO

**Version:** 1.0  
**Last Updated:** June 13, 2026  
**Status:** Active Development  
**Purpose:** Definitive visual bible for all major ships, motherships, and key units across all five playable races and enslaved minion species. Ensures artistic consistency, lore fidelity, and seamless integration with the full Powrush universe documentation.

---

## 1. Core Visual Philosophy

Powrush-MMO’s visual language is defined by **asymmetric faction identity** rooted in the **Draek Origin & The Great Betrayal**:

- **Quellorian / Ambrosian side**: Elegant, radial, luminous, resonance-driven. Clean hyper-advanced alien architecture with flowing auroral energy effects. Beauty as a byproduct of harmony.
- **Draek side**: Corrupted elegance. They stole Quellorian resonance technology centuries ago. Their ships retain graceful energy patterns and radial motifs, but twisted into oppressive, biomechanical, consumption-driven forms. Purple-red energy veins, jagged spikes, organic tendrils, and a sense of stolen beauty now used for domination.
- **Human side**: Patchwork scavenged tech. Modular, rugged, inventive, with glowing alignment-reactive elements.
- **Cydruid side**: Organic-tech fusion. Living wood-metal-crystal hybrids, root networks, symbiotic growth.
- **Enslaved Minions**: Tragic corrupted versions of their original aesthetics, with visible Draek biomechanical overrides (purple-red energy, control crystals, pain indicators).

**Key Rule**: Draek technology should feel like “Quellorian elegance, but wrong” — familiar radial symmetry and energy conduits, but corrupted, aggressive, and painful to look at.

## 2. Motherships

### The Auroral Unification Nexus (TAUN) — Quellorian

**Scale**: ~14+ km diameter  
**Primary Shape**: Massive central spherical core with 8–12 long, elegant radiating arms in perfect radial symmetry.  
**Color Palette**: Deep cosmic indigo to brilliant auroral blue-white. Gold and silver metallic accents.  
**Energy Effects**: Flowing auroral waves (blue-white) during high-energy operations, especially when deploying fleet or activating Resonance Burst. The arms glow brighter from base to tip.  
**Surface Details**: Clean hyper-advanced alien architecture. Subtle glowing resonance circuits. Large hangar bays integrated elegantly into the arms.  
**Signature Feature**: The entire ship produces visible aurora-like phenomena in space when operating at peak power — a natural byproduct of resonance field + plasma interaction.

**Art Direction Notes**:
- Protoss Carrier + Mothership hybrid, but more organic and majestic.
- Avoid overly aggressive spikes or dark tones.
- When in combat or Resonance Burst, the auroral effect intensifies dramatically.

---

### The Brood Spire (TBS) — Draek

**Scale**: ~12–15 km tall (vertically dominant)  
**Primary Shape**: Tall, ominous spire with a wide biomechanical base that tapers upward. Multiple segmented sections with organic-mechanical tendrils connecting them.  
**Color Palette**: Matte black and dark crimson with glowing purple-red energy veins. Gold circuitry patterns (stolen Quellorian influence).  
**Energy Effects**: Pulsing purple-red energy that travels along tendrils and spikes. Occasional “blood vessel” style flows when consuming or producing units.  
**Surface Details**: Aggressive spikes, biomechanical plating, visible consumption orifices, and control crystals embedded in the hull.  
**Signature Feature**: The vertical dominance and oppressive presence. It feels like a living throne of control.

**Art Direction Notes**:
- Heavily influenced by stolen Quellorian radial energy patterns, but twisted vertically and made aggressive.
- The gold circuitry should clearly echo Quellorian design language, but corrupted and used for domination.
- When the Hivelord is aboard or projecting power, the purple-red energy intensifies and tendrils become more active.

## 3. Quellorian Fleet Visual Language

**Overall Aesthetic**: Sleek, elegant, radial symmetry, luminous blue-white energy, clean advanced alien architecture.

### Aether-Class Interceptor
- Small, agile, dart-like with four elegant stabilizing fins.
- Bright blue-white energy trails.
- Subtle resonance glow along the hull.

### Luminar-Class Heavy Cruiser
- Medium size, balanced, with prominent forward resonance arrays.
- Two or three elegant arm-like structures.
- Strong radial symmetry in engine layout.

### Harmony-Class Support Carrier
- Larger, with multiple hangar bays integrated into a graceful central body.
- Soft glowing resonance fields around the hangars.
- Often seen with smaller ships in formation (visual harmony).

### Seraphim-Class Capital Escort
- Large, majestic escort with heavy resonance shielding visible as layered blue-white fields.
- Multiple elegant weapon arrays that look more like musical instruments than guns.

**Common Quellorian Visual Motifs**:
- Radial symmetry
- Clean glowing energy conduits
- Auroral particle effects
- Elegant, non-aggressive silhouettes

## 4. Draek Fleet Visual Language

**Overall Aesthetic**: Corrupted Quellorian elegance + biomechanical aggression. Purple-red energy, spikes, tendrils, consumption motifs. They look like they stole beauty and broke it.

### Swarm-Class Drone Fighter
- Small, aggressive, insectoid with stolen Quellorian energy core visible as purple glow.
- Jagged spikes, tendril propulsion.

### Ravager-Class Bio-Corvette
- Medium, fast, with prominent forward consumption maw.
- Purple-red energy veins pulsing along the hull.
- Clear visual echo of Quellorian radial design, but twisted and aggressive.

### Tyrant-Class Heavy Cruiser
- Large, imposing, with multiple segmented sections connected by tendrils.
- Heavy spike coverage, prominent control crystals.
- Strong vertical elements mixed with corrupted radial symmetry.

### Abomination-Class Capital Devourer
- Massive, horrifying, with a huge central consumption core.
- Multiple “mouths” and tendril arrays.
- Clear stolen Quellorian elegance in the energy patterns, now used for mass consumption.

**Common Draek Visual Motifs**:
- Purple-red energy veins (the “stolen resonance” signature)
- Aggressive spikes and tendrils
- Visible consumption orifices
- Gold circuitry that looks Quellorian but corrupted
- Pain/anger indicators (glowing redder when damaged or consuming)

## 5. Human Fleet Visual Language

**Overall Aesthetic**: Patchwork scavenged tech. Rugged, modular, inventive. Different ships look like they were built from salvaged parts of multiple factions.

**Key Features**:
- Visible welding seams, mismatched plating, jury-rigged systems.
- Glowing alignment-reactive elements (blue-white when leaning Quellorian, purple-red when leaning Draek, green-gold when leaning Cydruid, iridescent when leaning Ambrosian).
- Modular weapon and engine pods that can be visually swapped.
- Earth Defense Protocol ships have more unified, heroic, slightly retro-futuristic human military aesthetic with alien tech integrated.

**Signature Look**: “We took the best (and worst) from everyone and made it work.”

---

## 6. Cydruid Fleet Visual Language

**Overall Aesthetic**: Living organic-tech fusion. Wood-like metals, crystal growths, root networks, symbiotic glowing.

**Key Features**:
- Ships that look grown rather than built.
- Visible root systems and living groves integrated into the hull.
- Green-gold energy with soft bioluminescent accents.
- When using ecological abilities, visible growth and blooming effects.
- Strong connection to Sylvaris redemption visuals (healthy Cydruid ships look like what redeemed Sylvaris aspire to become).

---

## 7. Ambrosian Fleet Visual Language

**Overall Aesthetic**: Living crystalline lattice. Iridescent, geometric, resonance-driven.

**Key Features**:
- Ships made of living crystal lattices with internal glowing energy flows.
- Iridescent auroral effects (similar to TAUN but more geometric and crystalline).
- When Discordant: cracked crystal, purple-red corruption spreading through the lattice, painful dissonant glow.
- When redeemed/attuned: pure, harmonious, multi-colored resonance bloom.
- Lattice Crown structures on capital ships.

---

## 8. Enslaved Minion Species Visual Language

All enslaved species show visible Draek biomechanical overrides (purple-red energy, control crystals, pain indicators) layered over their original aesthetics.

### Veythari (Shattered Swarm)
- Original: Beautiful insectoid singers with harmonious wing patterns.
- Enslaved: Broken wings, jagged spikes, purple-red control crystals on thorax, agonized expressions.
- Redeemed: Restored harmonious wing patterns, blue-white resonance glow returning.

### Korrath (Broken Blades)
- Original: Noble four-armed honor-bound knights with elegant armor.
- Enslaved: Berserk armor covered in spikes and pain crystals, glowing red eyes, rage indicators.
- Redeemed: Armor restored to elegant form, honor sigils glowing, calm powerful presence.

### Sylvaris (Twisted Grove)
- Original: Peaceful plant symbiotes with beautiful living groves.
- Enslaved: Grotesque twisted roots, purple-red corruption veins, pain flowers, aggressive growth.
- Redeemed: Beautiful healthy groves returning, green-gold bloom, harmonious with Cydruid aesthetic.

### Luminari Exiles (Fallen Light)
- Original: Quellorian offshoot with elegant light-based forms.
- Enslaved: Corrupted elegant forms with heavy purple-red overlay, broken resonance circuits.
- Redeemed: Restored Quellorian elegance with powerful new hybrid light.

### Voidweavers (Shattered Mind)
- Original: Ancient energy beings of pure light and thought.
- Enslaved: Fragmented, chained, used as living psionic batteries with Crownstone crystals embedded.
- Redeemed: Reintegrated into beautiful lattice forms, powerful but stable.

## 9. Technical Implementation Notes (Bevy / Rendering)

- Use velocity_prepass + TAA pipeline for all ships to ensure buttery motion.
- Energy effects (auroral, purple-red veins, resonance glow) should be shader-driven with time-based animation.
- Draek purple-red energy should use a corrupted version of the Quellorian resonance shader (shared base with distortion).
- Human patchwork ships should have visible modular attachment points for hybrid protocol visual swapping.
- Cydruid and redeemed Sylvaris should have growth/bloom particle systems tied to ecological ability activation.
- Ambrosian crystal ships should have lattice refraction and internal energy flow shaders.
- All ships should react visually to Crownstone proximity (energy instability, color shift toward purple-red).
- Mirror Reckoning shadow versions should use desaturated + distorted versions of the server’s own ship aesthetics.

## 10. Development Priorities

1. Create high-fidelity concept art and 3D models for TAUN and TBS first (highest visual impact).
2. Establish clear material and shader libraries for Quellorian vs Draek energy effects.
3. Define modular kit for Human ships.
4. Create visual progression states for all five enslaved species (enslaved → redeemed).
5. Integrate visual feedback for Hybrid Instability and Pain Transmutation Paths.
6. Ensure all ships have clear silhouette readability at all zoom levels for dogfight gameplay.
