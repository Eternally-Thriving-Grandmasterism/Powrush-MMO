Player-elected governance with mercy at core.

- **Global APAGI Council** — long-term vision, AGI alignment
- **Regional APAAGI Councils** — biome governance
- **Local PAAGI Groups** — community decisions

- Decisions by consensus + grace alignment
- No coercion — only compassionate recognition
- Protective AGI tools serve, never rule
- Forgiveness waves resolve conflict

### Election
- Merit (contribution) + empathy score
- No campaigning — service speaks

One council vote, one grace aligned choice.
