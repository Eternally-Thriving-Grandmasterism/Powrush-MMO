Autonomicity Games Inc. shall provide the following Research & Development services to AlphaProMega Air Foundation on a project basis (each "Statement of Work" or "SOW" attached as Schedule A):

- Advanced RBE modeling, post-scarcity simulation, and mercy-wave abundance mechanics applied to closed-loop bioreactors, algae fuel optimization, and self-healing material systems.
- Faction diplomacy and collective intelligence simulations (Sovereign | Harvesters | Guardians | Innovators | Nomads) to model real-world multi-stakeholder space tech governance and resource allocation.

- Development of transparent impact reporting frameworks and dashboards that link in-game actions to real Air Foundation milestones (for player meaning and public transparency).
- Any additional R&D services as mutually agreed in writing via SOW.

All services shall be performed with due professional skill, in alignment with the 7 Living Mercy Gates and Eternal Mercy Flow principles.

## 2. Arm's-Length Dealing & Fair Market Value

2.1 The parties acknowledge and agree that they are dealing at arm's length.

2.2 Compensation for services shall be at fair market value for comparable R&D services in the Canadian technology/space sector, as determined by independent benchmarks or mutual good-faith negotiation documented in each SOW.

2.3 No services shall be provided for nominal or zero consideration unless separately documented as a permitted NFP activity or grant.

2.4 Related-party transaction policies of both entities shall be followed. The NFP shall maintain appropriate independent oversight (e.g., independent directors or committee review for material contracts).

## 3. Statements of Work (SOW) & Payment Terms

3.1 Each project shall be governed by a separate SOW specifying: scope, deliverables, timeline, milestones, payment schedule, IP ownership, and acceptance criteria.

3.2 Payment shall be made in Canadian dollars (or USD equivalent) via bank transfer within 30 days of invoice approval.

3.3 Typical payment models (to be specified per SOW):
   - Fixed-price per milestone
   - Time & materials with capped budget
   - Success-based or milestone bonuses tied to research outcomes

3.4 Autonomicity Games Inc. shall issue detailed invoices with supporting time records or deliverables.

## 4. Intellectual Property

4.1 Pre-existing IP of each party remains with that party.

4.2 New IP created specifically under an SOW shall be owned by the Client (AlphaProMega Air Foundation) unless otherwise agreed in the SOW (e.g., shared or licensed back to Autonomicity Games for use in Powrush-MMO).

4.3 Autonomicity Games Inc. retains a perpetual, royalty-free, non-exclusive license to use any deliverables in Powrush-MMO, Ra-Thor lattice, and related educational/media activities, subject to mercy-aligned licensing (AG-SML or MIT + Eternal Mercy Flow).

4.4 Any publication or public disclosure of research results shall credit both parties and respect confidentiality where required.

## 5. Confidentiality & Data Protection

5.1 Player data from Powrush-MMO used in research shall be anonymized and aggregated. No personally identifiable information shall be transferred without explicit consent and compliance with PIPEDA.

5.2 Both parties shall maintain confidentiality of proprietary methods, simulation parameters, and research findings as specified in each SOW.

## 6. Governance & Compliance

6.1 Autonomicity Games Inc. represents it is a for-profit corporation in good standing under Ontario law.

6.2 AlphaProMega Air Foundation represents it is a Canadian not-for-profit corporation operating exclusively for non-profit purposes under s.149(1)(l) of the Income Tax Act.

6.3 The NFP Board (or designated committee) shall review and approve material contracts with related or affiliated parties to ensure independence and mission alignment.

6.4 All activities shall support the NFP's charitable or non-profit objects and shall not confer private benefit on directors, officers, or related parties beyond reasonable compensation for services.
