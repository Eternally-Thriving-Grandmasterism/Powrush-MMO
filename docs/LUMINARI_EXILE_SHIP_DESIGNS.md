## 1. Lore Integration & Origin

The **Luminari Exiles** are a tragic splinter group of Quellorians who were captured during the early phases of the Draek invasion of Earth and surrounding systems. Once proud members of the Aetherion Luminari Alliance, they were subjected to the Crownstone’s psionic domination and forced into biomechanical augmentation.

Their ships reflect the **Great Betrayal**:
- They were originally built with pure Quellorian resonance technology (elegant radial symmetry, auroral energy).
- After capture, Draek engineers grafted hivemind nodes, consumption cores, and Crownstone relay crystals onto the hulls.
- The result is a haunting hybrid aesthetic: the beauty of Quellorian design corrupted by Draek predation and necessity-driven cruelty.

This makes Luminari Exile ships some of the most emotionally charged vessels in the game — players see what the Draeks did to their own kind.

## 2. Design Philosophy

**Core Visual Tension:**
- **Quellorian Elegance** (radial symmetry, flowing lines, glowing blue-white auroral energy) fighting against **Draek Corruption** (jagged biomechanical spikes, purple-red pulsing veins, exposed neural tendrils, cracked crystal lattices).
- The ships look *beautiful but wrong* — like classical sculptures covered in invasive growth.
- Enslaved state: Heavy asymmetrical additions, glowing Crownstone nodes, flickering inconsistent lighting.
- Redeemed state: Partial restoration of symmetry and light, with honorable "scars" (glowing gold-white seams where corruption was purged).

**Color Palette:**
- Enslaved: Deep void-black hulls with sickly purple-red energy fissures and corrupted blue-white highlights.
- Redeemed: Restored silver-white with vibrant auroral blue, accented by honorable gold seams and soft green growth (Cydruid influence during redemption).

## 3. Specific Ship Designs

### 3.1 Fallen Aether Interceptor (Light Attack / Scout)
**Enslaved Form:**
- Sleek Quellorian interceptor frame twisted with jagged biomechanical "wings" that twitch unnaturally.
- Single large Crownstone node on the dorsal spine that pulses with stolen resonance.
- Asymmetrical thruster arrays grafted from Draek Ravager parts.
- Visual tells: Flickering inconsistent engine glow, occasional purple energy arcs across the hull.

**Redeemed Form:**
- Clean radial symmetry restored.
- The Crownstone node is replaced by a pure Resonance Core that emits soft auroral trails.
- Thrusters become elegant and harmonious.
- Visual tells: Smooth flowing light patterns, soft harmonic glow when near other Quellorian ships.

**Gameplay Role:** Fast harassment, hit-and-run. Enslaved version has higher boarding vulnerability but also higher aggression.

---

### 3.2 Exiled Luminar Cruiser (Heavy Cruiser / Support)
**Enslaved Form:**
- Classic Quellorian radial cruiser body now covered in thick biomechanical plating and tendrils.
- Multiple Crownstone relay spines running along the main arms.
- A large "Consumption Bloom" sac on the ventral side that can launch boarding pods or resource-draining fields.
- The once-elegant central sphere is cracked and held together by glowing purple energy veins.

**Redeemed Form:**
- Radial arms partially restored with gold-white seams.
- Consumption sac replaced by a Harmony Amplification Chamber.
- Central sphere glows with stable auroral light.
- Gains Cydruid-inspired living grove plating on the hull for extra durability.

**Gameplay Role:** Mid-line support and boarding platform. Enslaved version spreads hivemind influence; redeemed version provides strong resonance buffs.

---

### 3.3 Broken Seraphim Escort (Capital Escort / Boarding Specialist)
**Enslaved Form:**
- Tall, elegant Quellorian escort frame now asymmetrical and aggressive.
- Large forward "maw" structure (Draek addition) used for ramming and boarding.
- Multiple tendril launchers along the hull.
- The ship constantly leaks purple-red energy and makes distorted harmonic sounds.

**Redeemed Form:**
- Symmetry and elegance return.
- The maw is sealed and replaced by a beautiful resonance projector.
- Tendrils become protective harmonic shields.
- The ship emits a soft, mournful but hopeful choral tone when moving in formation.

**Gameplay Role:** Aggressive boarding and escort. One of the most dangerous enslaved units; one of the most inspiring redeemed units.

---

### 3.4 Crownstone Relay Frigate (Unique Luminari Exile Class)
**Description:** A new hybrid class created by the Draeks specifically from captured Luminari crews and ships.
- Core is a heavily modified Quellorian frigate frame.
- Central feature is a massive exposed Crownstone crystal (the largest on any non-capital ship).
- The crystal is protected by biomechanical armor but leaks massive psionic energy.
- The ship acts as a mobile hivemind relay, boosting nearby Draek and enslaved units.

**Enslaved Form:** Extremely oppressive presence. The crystal pulses violently.
**Redeemed Form:** The crystal is purified into a Resonance Beacon. The ship becomes a powerful support platform that can project temporary harmony fields.

**Gameplay Role:** High-value target in dogfights and boarding. Destroying or redeeming it has major local impact on hivemind strength.

## 5. Integration with Core Systems

- **Dogfight Mechanics**: Enslaved Luminari ships have aggressive, somewhat erratic AI due to internal conflict. Redeemed ships fight with elegant precision and support nearby allies with harmony fields.
- **Boarding Mechanics**: Enslaved versions are easier to board (internal conflict weakens them) but more dangerous once boarded. Redeemed versions are harder to board but become powerful allies.
- **Redemption Quests** (The Fallen Light Returns): Players must board these ships during the questline. Visual state changes dramatically upon successful redemption.
- **Mirror Reckoning**: Servers with low mercy or high exploitation see more Luminari Exile ships in their Mirror, often as tragic, screaming shadows.
- **Hivelord Counter-Strategies**: The Hivelord prioritizes protecting or reclaiming these ships because they represent his greatest "success" in breaking Quellorian will.

## 6. Technical Implementation Notes

- **Shader & Material**: Base Quellorian PBR material with layered corruption mask. Use vertex animation for twitching tendrils and pulsing crystal nodes.
- **Particle Systems**: Auroral particles (blue-white) mixed with corrupted energy (purple-red). Redeemed ships emit soft floating light motes.
- **Velocity Prepass Compatibility**: All ships should have clean motion vectors. Corrupted energy effects should have slight velocity distortion to feel "unstable."
- **LOD Strategy**: High detail on Crownstone nodes and tendrils at close range. Simplified but still clearly corrupted at distance.
- **Event-Driven VFX**: On redemption, trigger a burst of purifying light that "heals" the hull cracks over 3–4 seconds.
- **Integration Hooks**: `LuminariExileState` component (enslaved/redeemed flag + corruption_level). Direct hooks into `simulation_integration.rs`, boarding systems, and VoiceDirector (distorted choral voice lines).
