## 1. Purpose of This Document

This document provides **specific, ready-to-record voice acting lines** for all major characters, playable races, and enslaved minion species in Powrush-MMO. It builds directly on `VOICE_ACTING_DIRECTION.md` and ensures every line feels maximally immersive, morally weighted, and coherent with the full lore (including the new **Draek Origin & The Great Betrayal**).

Lines are organized by:
- Character / Species
- Context (Idle, Combat, Redemption, Trilemma, Ritual, Mirror Reckoning, etc.)
- Emotional state / corruption level (for dynamic DSP processing)

All lines are written to support **11+ language localization** and tight subtitle sync.

### High Corruption / Desperation

- "Mother... why did you leave us to this?"

## 9. Technical Implementation Notes

- All lines are tagged with `context`, `emotional_state`, `corruption_level`, `redemption_phase`, and `species`.
- `VoiceDirector` resource selects lines based on current `WorldSimulationState`, `CrownstoneState`, `HivelordCounterStrategyState`, `GroveCommunionState`, etc.
- Dynamic DSP: 
  - Low corruption = clean + subtle harmonic layer
  - High corruption = increasing distortion, layered consumed voices, pitch fracture
  - Redemption = gradual restoration of clarity and beauty
- Lines are triggered via events from `simulation_integration.rs` (e.g., `ResonanceBurstEvent`, `PainTransmutationChosenEvent`, `MirrorReckoningPhaseEvent`).
- Priority system prevents voice spam during intense moments.
- All lines support real-time subtitle generation and 11-language localization.
