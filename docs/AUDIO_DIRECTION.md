## 1. Core Audio Principles

The audio direction of Powrush-MMO is built on the same philosophical and mechanical foundations as every other system in the game: **asymmetric faction identity**, **moral weight**, **mercy vs consumption**, and **deep technical integration** with the simulation layer.

### Guiding Principles

- **Asymmetry as Identity**  
  Quellorian audio is elegant, harmonic, crystalline, and transcendent. Draek audio is oppressive, biomechanical, guttural, and predatory. The player should *feel* which side they are aligned with through sound alone.

- **Moral & Emotional Weight**  
  Harmony creates beauty and abundance. Corruption and consumption create horror, desperation, and unease. Audio must make moral choices *audibly consequential*.

- **Adaptive & Reactive**  
  Music and soundscapes respond in real-time to the state of the Resonance Network, Hivemind strength, Crownstone integrity, Ambrosian attunement, and RBE abundance/scarcity.

- **Cinematic yet Functional**  
  Audio supports epic mothership moments and dogfight intensity while remaining clear for gameplay feedback (boarding windows, Resonance Burst readiness, hivemind surges).

- **Mercy-Aligned Soundscape**  
  Redemption and purification have hopeful, transcendent sonic payoffs. Corruption and domination have disturbing, tragic undertones. The audio always leaves space for grace and choice.

## 2. Music Direction

### Adaptive Score Architecture

Powrush-MMO uses a layered, state-driven adaptive music system:

- **Base Layer**: Faction-specific ambient drones and motifs that shift based on location (near TAUN, inside TBS, deep space, contested zones).
- **Intensity Layers**: Increase with dogfight density, boarding actions, Resonance Burst charge, or Hivelord interventions.
- **Event Layers**: Stingers, swells, and thematic explosions triggered by major simulation events.
- **Moral Layer**: Harmony-dominant music becomes more beautiful and choral; corruption-dominant music becomes more dissonant and industrial.

### Quellorian Music Identity

- **Core Palette**: Ethereal choirs (Ambrosian-inspired), crystal singing bowls, resonant chimes, auroral whooshes, clean futuristic pads, rising harmonic arpeggios.
- **Signature Motif**: A bright, ascending 7-note resonance theme that resolves into a major chord with subtle Ambrosian overtone singing.
- **Key Moments**:
  - **Resonance Burst**: Massive layered choir explosion + auroral swell that peaks with visual aurora effects. The music literally "sings" the harmony surge.
  - **Successful Purification / Redemption**: Hopeful, transcendent choral resolution with crystal harmonics.
  - **Auroral Sovereign Elyndor Presence**: Serene, authoritative, almost divine choral layers.

### Draek Music Identity

- **Core Palette**: Deep sub-bass drones, wet biomechanical squelches and pulses, psionic whispers, hive buzzing, bone-like percussion, corrupted choral fragments (twisted Ambrosian voices), aggressive industrial rhythms.
- **Signature Motif**: A descending, dissonant 5-note hivemind theme that fractures and multiplies.
- **Key Moments**:
  - **Hivelord Intervention / Crownstone Surge**: Sudden oppressive low-end hit + psionic scream + tendril sounds.
  - **Successful Boarding / Consumption**: Wet, grotesque, satisfying consumption sounds layered with dark satisfaction stinger.
  - **Discordant Ambrosian Outbreak**: High-pitched, shrieking, broken crystal sounds that feel painful and wrong.

### Crownstone Trilemma Music

The single most important musical moment in the game:

- **Destroy Path**: Tragic, shattering, resonant glass-break + fading choir into silence, then hopeful rebuild.
- **Capture & Repurpose Path**: Tense hybrid music that slowly resolves into a new, beautiful hybrid theme (Quellorian + purified Ambrosian elements).
- **Sabotage Path**: Slow-building dread with corrupted motifs that eventually triggers internal Draek civil war stingers.

## 3. Sound Design by Category

### Ambient & World Soundscapes

- **Deep Space / Neutral Zones**: Subtle stellar wind, distant cosmic chimes, faint resonance hum.
- **Quellorian Space (near TAUN or allied fleets)**: Clean harmonic drones, soft choral pads, auroral crackle (high-frequency crystalline texture).
- **Draek Space (near TBS or hivemind fleets)**: Low pulsing, wet organic movement, distant psionic whispers, occasional bone creaks.
- **Contested / High-Corruption Zones**: Dissonant interference, static-like psionic noise, increasing unease.

### Mothership Audio

**The Auroral Unification Nexus (TAUN)**
- Idle: Deep resonant hum with layered Ambrosian choir pads that swell gently.
- Active (fleet deployment, Resonance Amplification): Rising auroral whooshes + harmonic chimes that sync with visual aurora effects.
- Resonance Burst Charge: Building choir intensity that peaks with the visual burst.
- Boarding Defense: Sharp, elegant alarm chimes + precision resonance pulses.

**The Brood Spire (TBS)**
- Idle: Deep, wet, rhythmic biomechanical pulse + distant hive buzzing.
- Active (production, domination): Increasing wet squelches, tendril movement, psionic drone that grows oppressive.
- Hivelord Presence: Low, guttural, authoritative voice-like pulses from the Crownstone.
- Boarding Defense: Aggressive, wet, multi-layered swarm sounds + bone-cracking defenses.

### Fleet & Dogfight Audio

- **Quellorian Ships**: Clean energy whooshes, resonant engine hums, elegant weapon discharge (harmonic chimes + light impact), boarding pod launch (precise, high-tech).
- **Draek Ships**: Wet, organic engine rumble, jagged weapon fire (bio-plasma splatter + impact), swarm fighter buzzing that increases with density, boarding infestation sounds (wet tendril spreading).
- **Dogfight Intensity**: Dynamic mix that ducks music and amplifies combat sounds based on proximity and threat level. Resonance vs Hivemind interference creates audible "static" when the two fields clash.

### Boarding & Combat

- **Quellorian Boarding**: Precision resonance cutter sounds, elegant squad callouts, harmonic disruption pulses on success.
- **Draek Boarding**: Wet infestation, tendril penetration, consumption sounds, psionic domination whispers on success.
- **Crownstone Interaction**: Deep crystalline resonance when interacting with the artifact. Success/failure produces distinct shattering or attunement tones.
- **Resonance Burst during Boarding**: Massive harmonic explosion that overrides local soundscape.

### UI & Feedback

- **Resonance Meter / Harmony Level**: Subtle, beautiful crystalline chimes that increase in clarity and harmony as harmony rises.
- **Hivemind Strength Meter**: Low, oppressive pulse that grows more aggressive and distorted as hivemind strengthens.
- **Crownstone Integrity**: Tense, fragile crystalline tones that crack slightly as integrity drops.
- **Resonance Burst Ready**: Bright, rising harmonic chime with subtle choir layer.
- **Moral Choice Prompts (Trilemma)**: Neutral, weighty tonal stinger that feels consequential.
- **RBE Abundance Feedback**: Warm, abundant, harmonic reward sounds when harmony creates resources.
- **RBE Scarcity / Corruption Feedback**: Sharp, desperate, dissonant sting when consumption or corruption creates scarcity.

### VFX-Synced Audio

All major VFX have dedicated audio layers that sync perfectly:
- Auroral phenomena → whooshes + crystalline crackle
- Resonance Burst visual wave → massive harmonic swell + choir peak
- Hivelord tendril attacks → wet, aggressive, psionic impact
- Discordant Ambrosian corruption spread → painful, broken crystal shrieks
- Successful Redemption / Purification → hopeful, transcendent chime + soft choir resolution
- Dogfight explosions and impacts → faction-appropriate material responses (clean energy vs wet bio-matter)

## 5. Technical Implementation Notes

### Recommended Bevy Audio Stack

- **Primary**: `bevy_kira_audio` (recommended for advanced features: layered tracks, dynamic mixing, spatial audio, effects).
- **Fallback**: Bevy's built-in `bevy_audio` for simpler needs.
- **Spatial Audio**: Use `kira` spatial features or `bevy_kira_audio` + `bevy_egui` / custom for 3D positioning in dogfights and boarding.

### Event-Driven Architecture (Critical)

All audio must be triggered from the simulation layer, never hardcoded in visual systems:

```rust
// Example in simulation_integration.rs or dedicated audio system
fn play_resonance_burst_sfx(
    mut audio: ResMut<Audio>,
    resonance_state: Res<ResonanceNetworkState>,
    burst_events: EventReader<ResonanceBurstEvent>,
) {
    for event in burst_events.iter() {
        // Play layered choir + auroral swell based on burst strength
        audio.play(assets.resonance_burst_full)
            .with_volume(event.strength * 0.8 + 0.2)
            .with_pitch(1.0 + event.harmony_bonus * 0.1);
    }
}
```

### Key Global Simulation Resources for Audio

- `WorldSimulationState` (master)
- `CrownstoneState` (integrity, owner, corruption_level)
- `ResonanceNetworkState` (harmony_level, network_integrity, burst_cooldown)
- `DraekHivemindState` (hivemind_strength, local_zones)
- `AmbrosianAttunementState` + `DiscordantAmbrosianState`
- `AuroralSovereignState` + `HivelordState`

### Dynamic Mixing Recommendations

- **Harmony-dominant mix**: Boost high frequencies, choral layers, clean pads. Reduce low-end aggression.
- **Hivemind-dominant mix**: Boost sub-bass, wet mid-range, distorted elements. Duck beautiful elements.
- **Resonance Burst**: Temporary master bus "clarity" effect + reverb tail synced to visual aurora.
- **Boarding Success (Quellorian)**: Clean harmonic resolution sting.
- **Boarding Success (Draek)**: Wet, grotesque satisfaction layer.

### Performance & Scale Considerations

- Use object pooling for frequent combat sounds (laser whooshes, bio-impacts).
- Distance-based attenuation and occlusion for dogfights (especially important at mothership scale).
- Preload critical event sounds (Resonance Burst, Crownstone interaction, Hivelord voice).
- Consider procedural audio elements for large swarm densities (modulated hive buzz based on local drone count).

### Shader-Audio Sync (Future Polish)

- Resonance visual intensity can pulse in time with audio harmonics.
- Hivelord tendril VFX can react to psionic audio pulses.
- Crownstone crystal VFX can crack in sync with audio integrity tones.
