Permitted Strategic Entropy

In Powrush-MMO, "painful griefing" is technically permitted as part of the living RBE simulation. Players may take advantage of resources set up by others, form guilds that retaliate (e.g., setting fire to a third-party shipping system), sabotage, or use strategic deception through role-play (agents and double agents). This is intentional — servers that engage in heavy entropy will naturally progress slower in weekly Server Wars compared to more harmonious ones.

This is not chaos for chaos' sake. It is a training ground for real-world Resource-Based Economy dynamics.

Maximal Speech & Expression

We offer maximal freedom of speech and expression, inspired by X (Twitter) and similar open platforms. Global rules are kept simple and accepted everywhere. Unique Powrush-MMO servers in different jurisdictions may apply additional granular rules to honor the laws of their land.
